BTS ARMY Hub

BTS, an acronym of Bangtan Sonyeondan or “Beyond the Scene,” is a Grammy-nominated South Korean group that has been capturing the hearts of millions of fans globally since its debut in June 2013.

This is a website for the fans of BTS to learn more about the members of BTS and stay updated with their activities.

BTS consists of 7 members who play an important role in production and songwriting. They are also succesful solo artists who explore different genres of music.
